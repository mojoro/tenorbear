About

From Sonora
to the World Stage

Born in the sun-drenched landscapes of northern Mexico, Manuel Isaac Rodriguez discovered his voice in the churches and plazas of his homeland before bringing it to the concert halls of Europe.

Trained in the classical tradition with deep roots in the lyric tenor repertoire, Manuel brings an unmistakable warmth and emotional depth to every performance — from Puccini arias to beloved Mexican canciones that honor his heritage.

Now based in Berlin, he balances an active performance career with a dedicated voice teaching practice, helping singers at all levels discover the full potential of their instrument.
